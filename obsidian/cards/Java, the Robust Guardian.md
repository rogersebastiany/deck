## Card Text

> **[CONTINUOUS EFFECT]**
> This card gains 300 ATK for each "JVM Token" you control. If this face-up card is destroyed by an opponent's card effect (by battle or by card effect) and sent to the GY: You can Special Summon 1 "JVM Token" (Warrior/EARTH/Level 1/ATK 0/DEF 0) to your side of the field in Defense Position.

## Artwork Description
A heavily armored golden-bronze warrior with a glowing blue orb in his chest plate, holding a massive shield. Code streams in the background. The character wears sunglasses and has a beard.

## Synergies
- Generates JVM Tokens that boost its own ATK
- [[Kotlin, the Concise Warrior]] can be Special Summoned if this card is face-up
- Defensive wall for [[Python, the Script-Snake]] strategies
