## Card Text

> **[CIBER/EFEITO]**
> While this card is on the field, all "AWS" monsters you control can declare an attack during the same turn they are Normal or Special Summoned. If this card is destroyed by battle or card effect: you can add 1 "Edge Token" from your deck or GY to your hand.

## Artwork Description
A ghostly, luminescent humanoid figure made of pure blue energy, running at high speed across a red grid. Surrounded by floating CDN node icons. The embodiment of edge computing and content delivery — fast, ethereal, everywhere at once.
