## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> (Level 10/WATER) Cannot be Normal Summoned/Set. Must first be Special Summoned (from your hand) by banishing 2 monsters that mention "AWS" or "Cloud". Once per turn (Quick Effect): you can banish 1 card from your hand or face-up field; until the end of this turn, this card cannot be destroyed by battle or card effects, and you can change the Battle Position of 1 face-up monster. During the next turn, that position cannot be changed.

## Artwork Description
A towering golden-armored divine figure with an ornate crown and flowing ethereal energy. Similar golden premium rare border as [[Route 53 DNS Hydra]]. Blue crystalline energy radiates from its core. The ultimate database guardian — Aurora's relational power made manifest.

## Synergies
- Highest ATK in the deck at 4000
- Same summoning condition as [[Route 53 DNS Hydra]] — banish 2 AWS/Cloud monsters
- The ultimate beater of the [[Home#AWS Cloud Monsters|AWS archetype]]
- Only [[Ontological Tautology]] and [[Orthogonal Projection]] can stop it
- Shares golden status with [[Route 53 DNS Hydra]] and [[Ontological Tautology]]
