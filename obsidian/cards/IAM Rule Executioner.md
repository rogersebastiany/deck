## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> Once per turn: you can pay 1000 LP; target 1 face-up monster on the field. That target's effects are negated the end of this turn, and it cannot activate its effects. At the end of the damage step, if this card battled an opponent's monster: banish that monster.

## Artwork Description
A dark, menacing armored executioner figure with twin circular saw blades in each hand. Cards and documents scatter around it. Red glowing background with mechanical details. The embodiment of AWS IAM access control.

## Synergies
- Effect negation + banish makes it the ultimate removal tool
- Protects [[Cognito, HTTPSV2-GRPC Identity Guardian]] by removing threats
- Core piece for the [[Home#AWS Cloud Monsters|AWS archetype]] alongside [[EKS Kubernetes Orchestrator]]
- Can be summoned via [[Route 53 DNS Hydra]] or [[RDS Aurora Leviathan]] tribute
