## Card Text

> **[RESPONSE] (Counter Trap)**
> Activate when your opponent activates a card or effect that has already been activated during this turn. Negate that effect, and if you do, destroy that card.

## Artwork Description
A statue of Immanuel Kant in classical robes, chained by glowing red chains. Digital overlay showing: "MAXIM → UNIVERSALIZABILITY", "DUTY", "NON-CONTRADICTION". Mathematical and logical symbols (Σ, ∅, λ) float around. The philosopher stands on a red grid platform.

## Synergies
- Punishes repeated effect activation — strong vs combo decks
- Philosophy trap chain with [[Russell's Paradox]] and [[The Law of Excluded Middle]]
- Protects [[Descartes, the Existential Sorcerer]] from repeated negation attempts
