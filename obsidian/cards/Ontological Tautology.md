## Card Text

> **[RESPONSE]**
> (Counter Trap) Cannot be negated. This card cannot be destroyed. Activate when your opponent activates a card or effect. Negate that effect. Your opponent cannot activate cards or effects in response to this card. While this card is on the field, your "AWS" and "Cloud" monsters cannot be banished, returned to the hand or deck, or destroyed. Your opponent cannot change the field or hand size.

## Artwork Description
A golden divine figure standing before a glowing altar/tablet inscribed with "A is A" and "EXISTS BECAUSE IT EXISTS". The card radiates pure golden light. "A=A" formula displayed prominently. The ultimate tautological truth — a self-evident, self-justifying existence. Golden premium rare border. The most powerful card in the entire deck.

## Synergies
- **The ultimate card** — cannot be negated, cannot be destroyed, infinite stats
- Complete protection for all [[Home#AWS Cloud Monsters|AWS and Cloud monsters]]
- Locks opponent out of responses entirely
- Only a card like [[Russell's Paradox]] could thematically challenge its logic
- The philosophical apex: existence justified by existence itself
