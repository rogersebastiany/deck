## Card Text

> **[RESPONSE] (Counter Trap)**
> Activate when your opponent destroys the last monster you control. Special Summon 1 "Descartes Token" (Spellcaster/LIGHT/Level 1/ATK 0/DEF 0). If this Token is destroyed, your opponent gains 2000 Life Points.

## Artwork Description
Descartes meditating cross-legged on a red grid plane. Swirling blue text spirals around him: "COGITO", "SELF-AWARENESS", "ERGO", "I THINK". Empty set symbols (∅) and "null" scattered around. Ethereal, transcendent atmosphere.

## Synergies
- Creates the "Descartes Token" needed to summon [[Descartes, the Existential Sorcerer]]
- Last line of defense — activates when board is wiped
- Part of the Descartes combo chain with [[Descartes' Conscious Summon]]
