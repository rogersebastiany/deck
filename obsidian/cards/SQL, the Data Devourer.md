## Card Text

> **[EFFECT]**
> When this card is Summoned: You can target up to 2 cards in your opponent's Spell & Trap Zone; send them to the GY. This card cannot attack during the turn you activate this effect.

## Artwork Description
A massive armored machine-humanoid with SQL command plates embedded in its body: "SELECT", "WHERE", "DELETE FROM", "JOIN", "INDEX". Glowing blue core in chest. Database icons and key symbols border the card art.

## Synergies
- Spell/Trap removal clears the way for [[Kotlin, the Concise Warrior]] direct attacks
- Heavy hitter alongside [[Lisp, the Macro-Rendas Wizard]]
- Can clear [[Ontological Tautology]] if opponent controls it
