## Artwork Description
A red geometric projection space with mathematical formulas: P = A(A^T A)^{-1} A^T and v^T Pv = |Pv|^2. A large red "X" mark is projected onto a grid plane. Linear algebra projection diagrams with vectors and coordinate axes.
