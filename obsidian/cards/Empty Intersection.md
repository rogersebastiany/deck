## Card Text

> **[RESPONSE] (Counter Trap)**
> Activate when your opponent Special Summons a monster(s). Until the end of your opponent's next turn, your opponent cannot Special Summon monsters with the same Type or Attribute as that Summoned monster(s).

## Artwork Description
Two overlapping Venn diagram circles — one blue (Type: MACHINE, Attribute: DARK) and one red (Attribute: LIGHT, Type: REPTILE) — with an empty set symbol (∅) in the intersection. "A∩B=∅" displayed in all four corners of the art.

## Synergies
- Shuts down swarming strategies based on same Type/Attribute
- Devastating against [[Home#AWS Cloud Monsters|AWS Cloud Monsters]] (mostly MACHINE type)
- Set theory counter alongside [[Orthogonal Projection]] and [[Russell's Paradox]]
