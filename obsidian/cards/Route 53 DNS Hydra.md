## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> (Level 9/WATER) Cannot be Normal Summoned/Set. Must first be Special Summoned (from your hand) by banishing 2 monsters that mention "AWS" or "Cloud". Once per turn (Quick Effect): you can banish 1 card from your hand or face-up field; until the end of this turn, this card cannot be destroyed by battle or card effects, and you can switch the Battle Position of 1 face-up monster. During the next turn, that position cannot be changed.

## Artwork Description
A magnificent golden three-headed hydra dragon with ornate armor plating and a glowing blue cross symbol on its central head. Radiant golden light emanates from behind. The card itself has a golden/premium rare border. The multi-headed beast represents DNS resolution — routing queries to the correct destination.

## Synergies
- Requires 2 [[Home#AWS Cloud Monsters|AWS/Cloud monsters]] to summon (banish cost)
- Quick Effect protection makes it nearly unkillable
- Position switching disrupts opponent's battle strategy
- Shares golden status with [[RDS Aurora Leviathan]] and [[Ontological Tautology]]
- Can be tributed for [[Ontological Tautology]]
