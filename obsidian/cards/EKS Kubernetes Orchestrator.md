## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> (Level 8/LIGHT) While this face-up card is on the field, once per turn, if a "Pod" monster you control is destroyed by battle: you can Special Summon 1 "Pod Token" (Machine/LIGHT/Level 1/ATK 0/DEF 0) from your hand or deck. This card gains 500 ATK for each "Pod Token" on the field.

## Artwork Description
A massive robotic orchestrator with the Kubernetes logo on its chest. Surrounded by floating green cube "pods" and holographic deployment screens. "kubectl" interface visible. A smaller humanoid figure kneels before it.

## Synergies
