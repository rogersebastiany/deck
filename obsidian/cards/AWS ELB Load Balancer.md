## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> (Level 7/LIGHT) While this face-up card is on the field, load distribution targets and problems across the IT traffic. You control "load distribution". You can choose to distribute packets from your hand and move. This card gains 500 ATK for each "Pod Token".

## Artwork Description
A cybernetic octopus-like machine with a central blue glowing eye and multiple mechanical tentacles extending outward, distributing energy streams. Green and red data flows radiate from its core. The embodiment of elastic load balancing.
