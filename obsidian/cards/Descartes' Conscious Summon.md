## Artwork Description
An ascended version of Descartes, now standing with arms raised, radiating power. Same meditation imagery but with intensified red energy aura. Multiple glowing ∅ symbols. The philosopher has achieved full consciousness.

## Synergies

- Only [[Ontological Tautology]] can truly counter this card
