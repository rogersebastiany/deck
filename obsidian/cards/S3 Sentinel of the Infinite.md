## Card Text

> **[MACHINE/EFFECT]**
> (Level 8/EARTH) This card cannot be destroyed by card effects. Once per turn: you can target 1 "Policy" or "Statement" card in your GY or that is banished; protect it from being removed from that location until the end of the next turn. This card gains 500 ATK for each card under its protection.

## Artwork Description
A colossal stone-and-metal golem with the S3 bucket icon embedded in its chest core. Massive arms with hexagonal armored segments. "S3 bucket" labels on its joints. An immovable guardian of stored data — the ultimate defensive wall.
