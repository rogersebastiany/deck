## Card Text

> **[MONSTER EFFECT]**
> Level 6/LIGHT/Spellcaster. ATK/2400 DEF/2000.

> **[EFFECT]** Once per turn, you can pay 1000 Life Points to negate a Spell or Trap card activated by your opponent.

## Artwork Description
Same Descartes meditating figure as [[I Think, Therefore I Am]], but now framed as a golden-bordered monster card. Swirling "COGITO", "SELF-AWARENESS" text. Empty set symbols and "null" values surround him on the red grid.

## Synergies
- Summoned via [[I Think, Therefore I Am]]'s Descartes Token
- Spell/Trap negation protects your board
- Can evolve into [[Descartes' Conscious Summon]] for ultimate form
- Protected by [[Kant's Categorical Imperative]] against repeated removal
