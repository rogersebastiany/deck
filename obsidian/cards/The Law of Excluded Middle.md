## Card Text

> **[RESPONSE] (Counter Trap)**
> Activate when your opponent activates a card or effect that would create a new choice. If your choice (HEADS or TAILS) is correct, negate that effect and destroy that card.

## Artwork Description
A balance scale with "1 TRUE" on the left side and "0 FALSE" on the right side. The FALSE side is crashing down, shattering the ground. Red background with logical symbols (→, ↔, λ) and binary code. NAND and NOR gate labels visible.

## Synergies
- Coin-flip negation pairs with [[Russell's Paradox]] for trap-heavy strategies
- Can protect against effects that threaten [[Haskell, the Functional Alchemist]]
- Philosophy counter trap alongside [[Kant's Categorical Imperative]]
